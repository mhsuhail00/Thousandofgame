Metal Slug X PC

Metal Slug X PC Game is a very interesting game which is developed and published by SNK. In this game player has to play different levels and to accomplish each level he has to be very quick. There will be some enemies. Which may come across the player but he has to be conscious. At the start he may not be given the facilities and weapons and as he proceeds numerous weapons and tanks will be unlocked for later use. Health factor is also main point to proceed in the levels. Apart from weapons player will be given other opportunity like kick or jump etc to proceed efficiently. Bonus factor has also been added. Which may be given to the player

The Amazing Spider Man

Morality is used in a system known as “Hero or Menace”, where players will be rewarded for stopping crimes or punished for not consistently doing so or not responding. If the player responds to crimes and successfully stops them, their hero rating will go up, failing to do so will result in Spider Man’s rating to decrease. The player is able to upgrade Spider-Man’s web shooters. During certain sections of the game, the player can control Spider-Man’s alter ego Peter Parker and walk around doing detective work.
